Rainbow cake recipe

    Ingredients

  • 600g Self-raising flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 240g unsalted soft butter
  • 480g caster sugar
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 240g Semi-Skimmed milk
  • Food colouring red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet

    Instructions

  1. Preheat fan oven to 180c and line trays (I use an 6inch round tins, I baked 3 at a time)
  2. Cream the unsalted butter and caster sugar together until fully combined
  3. Sive the Self-raising flour and Baking powder together
  4. Mix Eggs, milk and vanilla extract together
  5. With a butter and caster sugar mixer on low alternate the dry and wet ingredients into this starting with dry and ending with dry
  6. Once combined evenly seperate the cake mix into seperate bowls (One bowl for every colour you are using)
  7. Bake for 25-30mins and allow to cool completely, repeat until all colours are baked
  8. Once all layers are cooled, level all the cakes
  9. Stack the layers using buttercream in between each layer, once stacked in your desiered choice use the buttercream to create a crumb coat to lock the crumbs in. Place in the fridge for 2hrs until hard to touch
  10. Once hardened have fun and decorate the cake in your choice of design
