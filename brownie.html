Chocolate orange brownie

    Ingredients

  • 200g Dark Chocolate
  • 200g Unsalted Butter
  • 3 Large free range eggs
  • 275g Light brown sugar
  • 90g Plain flour
  • 45g Coco Powder
  • 200g frozen Chocolate orange
  • 100g milk Chocolate chips (optional)
  • 100g white Chocolate chips (optional)

    Instructions

  1. Preheat fan oven to 160c and line tray (I use an 8inch square tin)
  2. Melt the dark Chocolate and unsalted butter and leave to cool to room temprature
  3. Once cooled, whisk eggs and sugar in a large mixing bowl until doubled in size
  4. Add flour and coco powder to the eggs and sugar mix
  5. Once combined fold in the cooled melted butter and chocolate mix
  6. Fold in milk and white chocolate chips (if using)
  7. Pour half the mix into the tin, add the frozen chocolate orange to cover the wet mixture, pour the remaining mix on top
  8. Bake for 25-30mins and allow to cool completely before cutting into 12 sections
